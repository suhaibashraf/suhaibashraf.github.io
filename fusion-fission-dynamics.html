Fusion Fission Dynamics

Home Fusion Fission Dynamics

Developed Netlogo and Thymio robotics projects as a part of coursework for the course Artificial Life with Robotics. The robot followed other robots, walk in flocks, detected the obstacles or line and avoided.

I divided the detection zones into three different layers far sight, near sight and perspective space. By changing the values of these variables, the robots changes their behaviour like following other robot or walk in flock of robots.
